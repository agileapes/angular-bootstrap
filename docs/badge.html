Badge

Badges are helpful in adding a contextually meaningful piece of information to a pre-existing component.

Example

4
  • 5 Home
  • new Home 
<ui:badge>4</ui:badge>
<ui:badge><ui:icon glyph="new-window"></ui:icon></ui:badge>
<div style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
    <ul class="nav nav-pills nav-stacked">
        <li class="active">
            <a href="#">
                <ui:badge>5</ui:badge>
                Home
            </a>
        </li>
        <li class="active">
            <a href="#">
                <ui:badge class="pull-right">new</ui:badge>
                Home
            </a>
        </li>
    </ul>
</div>
Take a look at the icon directive, which goes nicely with buttons.
