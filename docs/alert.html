Alert

Alerts help you signify a results or a critical piece of information which you need to share with your users.

Example

You have new messages in your inbox. Remove the /install/ directory for security 
<ui:alert type="information">
    You have new messages in your inbox.
</ui:alert>
<ui:alert type="warning" dismissible="true">
    Remove the <code>/install/</code> directory for security
</ui:alert>
Take a look at the icon directive, which goes nicely with buttons.
