Label

Labels are fancy visual components that let you easily grab the attention of your users, while presenting essential bits of information in as an abstract a way as possible.

Example

No type Default Primary Success Information Warning Danger Danger 
<ui:label>No type</ui:label>
<ui:label type="normal">Default</ui:label>
<ui:label type="primary">Primary</ui:label>
<ui:label type="success">Success</ui:label>
<ui:label type="information">Information</ui:label>
<ui:label type="warning">Warning</ui:label>
<ui:label type="danger"><ui:icon glyph="ban-circle"></ui:icon> Danger</ui:label>
<ui:label type="danger">Danger</ui:label>
Take a look at the icon directive, which goes nicely with buttons.
