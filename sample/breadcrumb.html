Breadcrumbs

Creating breadcrumbs for navigating through nested pages is absolutely essential.

Here is a sample for a breadcrumb component:

Home Library Books View Book

Which directly results from the code:

<ui:breadcrumbs>
    <ui:breadcrumb glyph="home" href="javascript:alert('home');">Home</ui:breadcrumb>
    <ui:breadcrumb glyph="list" href="javascript:alert('library');">Library</ui:breadcrumb>
    <ui:breadcrumb glyph="book" href="javascript:alert('books');">Books</ui:breadcrumb>
    <ui:breadcrumb glyph="eye-open" href="javascript:alert('view book');">View Book</ui:breadcrumb>
</ui:breadcrumbs>

If you try, you will notice that the href attribute on the last breadcrumb will be completely ignored, as it is currently the state in which the application resides, and will not be allowed to elicit any sorts of hyperlinks.
